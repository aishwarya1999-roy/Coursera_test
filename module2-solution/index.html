Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Sandwich

A sandwich is a food typically consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread, or more generally any dish wherein bread serves as a container or wrapper for another food type. The sandwich began as a portable, convenient finger food in the Western world, though over time it has become prevalent worldwide.

Samosa

Samosa is a India Snack, we can call it a snack, an appetizer, an entree, or a full dinner unto itself. All of these can be achieved depending on how the samosa is prepared and presented! Some of the earliest mentions of what we know of today as the samosa can be found in Persian writings dating back to the 9th century. It is a fried dough with a smashed potato stuffing.
